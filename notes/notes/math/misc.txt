====Miscellaneous Notes====

==Fixed Ratio Money Management==

$$ N = 0.5 \times \left ( \sqrt{1+8\frac{P}{\Delta}} +1 \right ) $$

where, \\ 
N = Number of Contracts \\
P = Account Profit to Date \\
$ \Delta $ = $ amount needed to trade another contract \\


==Logic==
$$ p \rightarrow q \Leftrightarrow \neg p \vee q $$
$$ \neg p \rightarrow  \neg q \Leftrightarrow q \rightarrow p $$
$$ \neg p \rightarrow q \Leftrightarrow p \vee q $$
